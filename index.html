Who we are

About Us

Zivai Community Empowerment Trust (ZICET) is a Community Based Organisation (CBO) that was established in 2015 by women and youths in Mutare and Mutasa Rural District after realising that most women and youths are being left out in the economic development agenda.

Women and Youth exclusion was noted as being most pronounced in the mining sector which is an important pillar to the economic development of Zimbabwe.

Our Mission

To provide and create a platform for women and youth to identify and utilise their potential for the meaningful development of their local areas through community based natural resources management. We seek to promote equitable and inclusive participation of marginalised communities in attaining social, economic, and environmental just development.

Our Vision

Youth, Women and People with Disabilities who Prosper and Enjoy their Rights to Natural Resources.

We believe access to information on resources and benefits that accrue from public resources as well as information on development plans is crucial for every citizen to access. We strongly seek for natural resources integrity to spur development of mining host communities.
